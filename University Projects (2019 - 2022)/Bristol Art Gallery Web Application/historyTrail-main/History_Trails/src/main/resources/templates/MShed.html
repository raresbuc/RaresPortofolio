M Shed

M Shed is a museum all about Bristol. It tells the story of the city and its unique place in the world. Explore the city through time: its places, its people and their stories by viewing the colourful exhibits detailing history of the city and its people. See amazing film and photographs, listen to moving personal stories, encounter rare and quirky objects and add your own memories of Bristol through the interactive displays.

The Bristol Places gallery focuses on the physical and dynamic city– the ways people have shaped it and continue to do so.

Bristol People
The Bristol People gallery explores activities past and present that make Bristol what it is – creating, trading, challenging and celebrating.

The Bristol Life gallery explores people’s shared experiences in the city, from the momentous to the everyday – see, hear and share stories.
